ACHIEVEMENTS

SYM STARTUP GOT AQUIHIRED

Hearty congratulations to team SYM. Wish you the best of life ahead!

ACCS-ARM Design Challenge 2016

Team comprising of Eldhose Joy, Varghese Mathai and Arun Baby bagged the third prize of Rs 40,000, for their work on “Zone Adaptive Warning and Speed Control System”in the finals of 3rd National Level ACCS-ARM Design challenge at Hosur, Karnataka by Competing among the top 14 teams across Nation.

ACCS-ARM Design Challenge 2016

The team led by KPS Sachin, Athul Stanley and Abhijith A Sabu and mentored by Prof. Anas M.M received the honour of ‘Most Promising Project’ in the 3rd National Level ACCS-ARM Design for their effort On “Driving Control System Based on Heart Beat” and won Rs. 25,000/- for their project.

Paper Presentation

The mini-project team consisting of Sruthy Chandran, Soniya Sanu, Rekha Narayanan and Anju Saji, S6 CSE 2017 , under the guidance of Asst. Prof Divya Madhu, presented a paper on ”OMALISTA:An user assistance to rack up tagged wish car” at IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Control Systems (ICICCS 2017) organized on 15-16, June 2017 by The Vaigai College Engineering (VCE), Madurai

TEDxMITS
<

Having conceived with an aim to provide a platform for the TEDx enthusiasts from Kerala, TEDxMITS that took place on Saturday, July 23 became an event that showcased the innovative ideas of many eminent speakers. The event took place at Muthoot Institute Of Technology and Science, Kerala and had many brilliant personalities as speakers and had talks on a variety of topics that ranged from the basic and local environmental concerns to the discussions on Karnatic grammar. The attendees had the opportunity to interact with the speakers and share their ideas. The event was organised in such a way that there were many interactive sessions that made it very lively and interesting.
